Troubleshooting

App can't reach the Orin
Ping test from phone is useless — just toggle tether → hotspot. If both fail and you have cell signal, tell Claude; gateway-vs-Orin triage is documented and remote.
GPS stale mid-session
GPS card → Ingest restart button. Fixes the stale-serial-after-Wio-reboot case in ~10 s.
Service wedged / app shows stale everything
Tools → Service → Restart. The new watchdog also auto-recovers a wedged service within 2 min on its own.
Camera won't move at all
Check E-stop state in the app first — a latched stop looks identical to a fault.
