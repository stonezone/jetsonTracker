The target (the architecture I'd own)

One sentence: a world-frame target estimator at the center — GPS fixes and vision detections are just weighted measurements, the controller always points at the estimate, zoom widens with uncertainty — wrapped in closed-loop pointing, full solo-operation tooling (phone + wrist + on-body buzzer), deterministic self-healing, and a codebase a stranger could inherit.

What gets deleted on arrival: the TrackingArbiter, the release-before-request handoff dance, and the GPS-cue boost — all bridges to be burned once the estimator flies. The sequencing below exists because the estimator must be tuned against real session telemetry, which only Phases 1-2 can produce.
