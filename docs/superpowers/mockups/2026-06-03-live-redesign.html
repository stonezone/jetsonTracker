One cohesive glass rail (~62pt) replaces the 5 stacked cards — icon buttons for fullscreen, zoom ±, AUTO, REC, HOME, and a red STOP pinned at the bottom. More conservative; keeps a dedicated control strip but slim + unified.

◎ AUTO — single toggle for autonomous tracking (replaces Start Auto + Stop PTZ) ● REC — start/stop recording ⌂ HOME — recenter (also tap joystick center) ⤢ — fullscreen STOP — Emergency Stop / KILL, always visible
Also changing regardless of option: the standalone PTZ tab is removed (this screen is the only Live+PTZ surface → clean 5 tabs). Errors/refusals surface as a brief glass toast over the feed instead of a permanent pill. Portrait uses the same language: feed on top, the glass dock + zoom rail float, no boxed card stack. Liquid Glass falls back gracefully to a translucent dark material on older iOS.
